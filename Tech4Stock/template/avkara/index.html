What is AVKARA?

AVKARA is a waste management company by Tech4Stock. This applicationis a online waste selling portal ehich lets the users choose which vendor hey wish to sell thir waste. Customers can also choose when to sell their waste, according to their meeds.

Why AVKARA?

Currently, there is no service that lets you sell your waste to the available vendors. AVKARA lets you do that. We all have faced a time when have desired a raddiwala on our doorstep but were unsucessful finding him on the right time. AVKARA help you sell your waste by letting you call your desired waste vendor, from the desired vendors list, who can pickup your waste at the rate that suits you.

What is the meaning of the word, 'AVKARA'?

AVKARA is a sanskrit word meaning, 'dustbin'. It is relatable to the work we do :)
